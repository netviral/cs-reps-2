## Overview  
The **AWS Certified Machine Learning – Specialty** certification demonstrates advanced knowledge in designing, building, and deploying ML models in the cloud using AWS services such as SageMaker and Lambda.

---

## Recommended Background  
Students who excel in:  
- **Introduction to Machine Learning (IML)**  
- **Data Science and Management (DSM)**  
- **Advanced Machine Learning**  
- **Cloud Computing (if available)**  

will have the right mix of theory and applied skills for this exam.

---

## Skills You’ll Gain  
- Building scalable ML pipelines  
- Automating model training and tuning  
- Deploying models to production  
- Monitoring and optimizing model performance  

---

## Exam Details  
- **Format:** 65 questions (MCQ and multiple response)  
- **Duration:** 180 minutes  
- **Mode:** Online or in-person testing center  

---

## Fees and Validity  
- **Fee:** $300 (~₹25,000)  
- **Validity:** 3 years
