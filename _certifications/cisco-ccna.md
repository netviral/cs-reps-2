## Overview  
The **Cisco CCNA** certification is the global standard for entry-level network engineers. It validates the ability to install, configure, and troubleshoot medium-sized routed and switched networks.

---

## Recommended Background  
Students with strong performance in:  
- **Computer Networks (CN)**  
- **Operating Systems (OS)**  
- **System Security and Privacy**  
- **Information Security (InfoSec)**  

---

## Skills You’ll Gain  
- Network access and IP routing  
- Security and automation fundamentals  
- Basic infrastructure services  
- Device configuration and troubleshooting  

---

## Exam Details  
- **Exam Code:** 200-301 CCNA  
- **Format:** MCQ + simulation questions  
- **Duration:** 120 minutes  
- **Passing Score:** ~825 / 1000  

---

## Fees and Validity  
- **Fee:** $300 (~₹25,000)  
- **Validity:** 3 years
