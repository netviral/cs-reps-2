## Overview  
The **CompTIA Security+** certification is a great entry point for students aiming to build a career in cybersecurity. It validates hands-on skills in assessing and managing security risks, securing networks, and responding to incidents.

---

## Recommended Background  
Students excelling in these courses will find Security+ easier to pursue:  
- **Information Security (InfoSec)**  
- **Computer Networks (CN)**  
- **System Security and Privacy**  
- **Operating Systems (OS)**  

---

## Skills You’ll Gain  
- Threat analysis and mitigation  
- Risk management and compliance  
- Secure network architecture  
- Cryptographic techniques  
- Access management  

---

## Exam Details  
- **Format:** MCQs and practical performance tasks  
- **Duration:** 90 minutes  
- **Questions:** Up to 90  
- **Passing Score:** 750 / 900  

---

## Fees and Validity  
- **Exam Fee:** $404 (~₹34,000)  
- **Validity:** 3 years (renewable through Continuing Education Units)

## Career Outlook  
Ideal for roles such as:  
- Security Analyst  
- Network Administrator  
- Security Operations Center (SOC) Technician  
- Junior Penetration Tester  

Security+ provides the baseline security skills required across IT roles and organizations globally.
