Roshni Agarwal, a Computer Science student at Ashoka University, has been awarded the **ESET Women in Cybersecurity Scholarship**, an international recognition that celebrates women excelling in cybersecurity and information technology.  

The scholarship is presented by **ESET**, a global leader in digital security, to encourage greater representation of women in the cybersecurity field. Roshni’s project focused on developing secure communication protocols and analyzing network vulnerabilities, earning high praise from the review committee.

This achievement highlights not only her technical excellence but also her commitment to advancing **digital safety and privacy**.  

> “Receiving this scholarship reinforces my belief that cybersecurity is not just about systems, but about people — protecting their data, their identities, and their digital freedoms,” said Roshni.

Roshni plans to use the scholarship to further her research in ethical hacking and network defense. She encourages more young women to take up roles in the cybersecurity domain, emphasizing that diversity brings stronger and more innovative security solutions.

Congratulations, Roshni, on this incredible milestone!
